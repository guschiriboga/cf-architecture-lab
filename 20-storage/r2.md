# R2 (Object Storage)

> **Docs:** https://developers.cloudflare.com/r2/

## Summary
S3‑compatible object storage with **egress to the Internet priced at $0** for Standard tier.

## Free Tier (selected)
- **Storage:** 10 GB‑month
- **Class A ops:** 1,000,000 / month
- **Class B ops:** 10,000,000 / month
- **Egress:** Free to Internet (Standard tier)
